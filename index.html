Hospital Patient Care

Hospital patient care aides work as health care assistants specifically in a hospital setting. They work under the supervision of and take direction from staff nurses and doctors. Typical duties of a hospital patient care aide include taking hospital patients' temperature, blood pressure, pulse, and respiration; assisting patients with basic tasks, such as bathing, dressing, and eating; preparing and administering some medications; collecting specimens for lab tests; monitoring patients, and recording treatment.

Bouncers

They monitor people by checking I.D.s and cover charge at the door, make sure nobody is acting aggressively or destructively, and protects property and equipment from damage.
